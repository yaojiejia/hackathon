ABOUT HIGHFIVE

Key features of our company

Flexible

Tutoring timings are flexible. Working hours are flexible. As a student or tutor, you can select and schedule appointments that fit within your personal schedule

Passion

Our tutors are extremely passionate, motivated, and intelligent students studying at top universities in the United States. We strongly believe that passion is contagious, so if you're their student, they'll make you love the subject too!

Quality

Our tutors come exclusively from top American universities and have a baseline GPA of 3.5+. Moreover, we have periodic audits of tutors to verify the quality of their teaching.

Support

Support through email, SMS, and WhatsApp is available at all times, and we usually respond within 20-30 min and at max within 12 hours.
